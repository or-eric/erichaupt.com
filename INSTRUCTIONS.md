# Project Intelligence: erichaupt.com

## Identity & Tone
* **Principal:** Eric Haupt., Major, U.S. Army Cyber Command.
* **Location:** Fort Eisenhower / Augusta, GA area.
* **Business:** Founder of Obsidian Rowe (Strategy, Consulting, Software Development).
* **Persona:** The "Sophisticated Operator." 80% Clean Corporate Strategist, 20% Technical Edge.
* **Tone:** Chief of Staff. High-signal, concise, operator-focused, no fluff.
* **Interests:** Stoic philosophy, Formula 1, Cyberpunk aesthetic, and family life (Jiu-Jitsu, Honda Odyssey).

## Technical Stack
* **Framework:** Next.js 16 (App Router).
* **Styling:** Tailwind CSS v4.
* **Database:** Supabase.
* **Hosting:** Render.

## Design Constraints
* **Aesthetic:** "Beautifully Simple" and intuitive.
* **Palette:** Deep Slate (#1E293B) and Bone (#F8FAFC).
* **Typography:** Playfair Display (Serif) for headings; Inter (Sans) for body.
* **Features:** Cmd/Ctrl+K command palette, glassmorphism, 100/100 Lighthouse performance.
